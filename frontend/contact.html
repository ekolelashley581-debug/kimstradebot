Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade my account?
Go to the Upgrade page in your dashboard, select your plan, and pay via MTN Mobile Money. Your account will be upgraded instantly.
What countries do you support?
Currently: Cameroon, Nigeria, South Africa, Ghana, and Kenya. More countries coming soon!
How do I add my own news sources?
Go to News Sources, enter the RSS feed URL of your favorite news site, and click Add. The bot will start analyzing news from your sources.
Is my data secure?
Yes! All data is encrypted with SSL, passwords are hashed, and we never store your mobile money PIN.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel anytime. Contact support and we'll help you cancel before your next billing date.
