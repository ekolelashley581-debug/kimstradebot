Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kim's Trade Bot. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our trading platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Email address: Used for account creation and communication
  • Phone number: Required for MTN Mobile Money payments and account recovery
  • Country of residence: To provide local pricing and payment methods
  • Language preference: To customize your experience

2.2 Payment Information

  • All payments are processed securely through MTN Mobile Money
  • We do not store your mobile money PIN or banking details
  • Transaction records are kept for accounting purposes

2.3 Usage Data

  • Trading preferences and analysis history
  • News sources you've selected
  • Feature usage statistics to improve our service

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our trading analysis services
  • To process your payments and manage your subscription
  • To personalize your experience and show relevant content
  • To communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support
  • To improve our AI analysis and trading algorithms

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted using SSL/TLS technology
  • Passwords are hashed and cannot be read by anyone
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • MTN Mobile Money: Payment processing (subject to MTN's privacy policy)
  • TradingView: Live charts and market data
  • News API: For fetching market news (when you use our sources)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal or accounting purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
