Our Story

Kim's Trade Bot was born from a simple observation: African traders were being left behind by global trading platforms. Complex payment systems, lack of local support, and tools designed for Western markets made it difficult for African traders to compete.

We started in Cameroon, building a C++ trading engine that could handle local market conditions. Then we added MTN Mobile Money payments, making it easy for anyone with a phone to subscribe. As we grew, we expanded to Nigeria, South Africa, Ghana, and Kenya.

Today, Kim's Trade Bot serves thousands of traders across Africa, providing professional-grade analysis at prices locals can afford. And we're just getting started.
